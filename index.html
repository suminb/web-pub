Sumin Byeon

His passion lies in distributed systems and machine learning. He also likes to argue that math can be advantageous in everyone's daily lives. That's probably because he studied Computer Science and Mathematics in college. His most beloved language was Python, but ever since he wrote MMORPG server code in Python, he is in a love-and-hate relationship with the language as he discovered some dark side at the deepest part.
